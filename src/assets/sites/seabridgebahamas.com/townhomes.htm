Phase One of Seabridge enjoys an extraordinary elevation with spectacular ocean views. It is comprised of luxury townhomes ranging in size from 3690-4320 square feet of finished, air-conditioned space. Expansive, two-level covered balconies provide dramatic, outdoor living space overlooking the world-renowned, crystal clear waters of The Bahamas. The 3-bedroom townhomes offer a two-car garage, and the 4-bedroom townhomes offer a one-car garage. Superior finishes and features are all standard in our residences.
